About Broughton

Broughton is a village in Flintshire, Wales, known for its blend of historical charm and modern industry. Located near the border with England, it boasts a rich history, with roots stretching back to the medieval period.

In recent years, Broughton has become synonymous with aerospace innovation due to the presence of the Airbus UK factory, a major employer in the area.

Despite its industrial advancements, Broughton maintains a close-knit community atmosphere, characterised by its local shops, schools, and green spaces, making it a unique blend of the past and the future.
