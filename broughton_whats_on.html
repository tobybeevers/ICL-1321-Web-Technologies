Broughton What's On!

Explore our beautiful village and discover local attractions, historical landmarks, and scenic spots that make Broughton a delightful place to visit.

St. Mary's Church: Visit this historic church, a cornerstone of our community, known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.

Broughton Shopping Park: Enjoy a day of shopping and dining at our modern retail park, featuring a variety of stores and eateries to suit all tastes.

Community Walks: Take in the fresh air and stunning scenery with our numerous walking trails, perfect for a leisurely stroll or a brisk hike.
